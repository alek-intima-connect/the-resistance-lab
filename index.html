The Canon

"To build the new world, we must understand the code of the old one."

I. The Ancients (The Soul)

The Republic

PLATO // PHILOSOPHY

Essential for understanding the Allegory of the Cave. The reality presented to us by media and corporations is a shadow on the wall. We must have the courage to walk into the sun.

Nicomachean Ethics

ARISTOTLE // VIRTUE

The guide to Eudaimonia. We learn that the ultimate goal of human existence is not profit, but the virtuous action of "living well."

II. The Theorists (The Structure)

Das Kapital

KARL MARX // ECONOMICS

A technical manual on the mechanics of Surplus Value. It explains how your time is converted into private capital and provides the economic justification for The Reclamation.

The Conquest of Bread

PYOTR KROPOTKIN // ANARCHISM

The definitive argument for Mutual Aid. We learn that competition is a survival strategy for the weak, while cooperation is the strategy of the strong.
